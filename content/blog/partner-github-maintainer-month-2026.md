Open-source maintainers are the **backbone of the software world**, quietly dedicating their time and expertise to keeping the tools we all rely on alive and well. We are thrilled to once again partner with GitHub for **Maintainer Month**, joining a community-wide effort to recognize and support their invaluable work.

## What is Maintainer Month?

**Maintainer Month** is GitHub's annual initiative to **celebrate** and **uplift** open-source maintainers around the world. It's a reminder that the software industry stands on the shoulders of thousands of volunteers and independent contributors who keep critical projects healthy.

This year marks the **fifth edition** of Maintainer Month, and the energy around it keeps growing. The month is filled with events, discussions, and resources designed to help maintainers strengthen their projects and connect with the broader community. Check out the [Maintainer Month website](https://maintainermonth.github.com/) for the full schedule and available resources.

## Mockoon and open source — still going strong

Mockoon has been a **free and open-source API mocking platform** since 2017, and we are as committed to that mission as ever. Our goal has always been to give developers reliable, accessible tooling without gatekeeping it behind a paywall. We believe open source is one of the most powerful forces driving software innovation forward.

Sustaining an open-source project over the long haul is no small feat. It takes time, dedication, and, increasingly, financial resources. We've written openly about our journey toward [building a sustainable model](/blog/our-commitment-open-source-community/) while [keeping Mockoon free and open-source](/blog/seven-years-2024-retrospective/#mockoon-cloud-and-the-path-to-sustainability). We're glad to say that effort is paying off — and it puts us in a position to give back.

That's exactly what we want to do through Maintainer Month this year.

![Mockoon Cloud{512x512}](/images/blog/partner-github-maintainer-month-2026/maintainer-month-tools.png)

## Free Mockoon Cloud for open-source maintainers

For the second year running, we are partnering with GitHub to offer **free Mockoon Cloud accounts to open-source project maintainers**. Whether you need shared API mocks for your team, a stable environment for integration testing, or simply a better way to collaborate on API design, Mockoon Cloud has you covered.

Getting access is straightforward: fill out [this form](/mockoon-cloud-open-source/) with a few details about your project, and once reviewed, you'll receive a free account you can put to work right away.

![Mockoon Cloud{1108x951}](/images/blog/partner-github-maintainer-month-2026/maintainer-month-partner-pack.png)

You can also browse the [Maintainer Month Partner Pack](https://maintainermonth.github.com/partner-pack) to discover all the other offers available from this year's partners.

Open-source maintainers give so much to the community. It's only right that we find ways to give something back. We hope free access to Mockoon Cloud can remove at least one friction point from your workflow and let you focus on what matters, building great software.

Help us **spread the word** and celebrate the people who keep open source alive!
